## 0.1.0

- Initial release.
- Pure Dart core: `prepare`, `layout`, `prepareWithSegments`, `layoutWithLines`,
  `walkLineRanges`, `layoutNextLine`.
- Flutter convenience layer: `TextPainterMeasure`, `prepareForStyle`,
  `prepareWithSegmentsForStyle`.
- `WhiteSpace.normal` and `WhiteSpace.preWrap` modes.
- Hard newline support.
- Variable per-line width via `layoutNextLine`.
